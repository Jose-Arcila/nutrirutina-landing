# Refined Offer Strategy & Upsells

Yes — I’d structure Nutrirutina exactly like Sonhora: simple entry offer, strong main offer, premium accompaniment, and small upsells.

The key is to avoid selling “a diet PDF.” The product should be:

A personalized nutrition plan plus an execution system.

That lets you charge more than generic meal plans.

## Suggested Nutrirutina offers

### 1. Plan Inicial — 150.000 COP
For people who are curious but not ready to pay 300k.
**Positioning:** A first personalized guide to understand what to improve and how to start.
**Includes:**
* Initial intake form
* Review of habits, goals, restrictions, schedule, and food preferences
* General personalized nutrition recommendations
* Basic daily meal structure
* Practical first-step suggestions
* Delivery as PDF or WhatsApp summary
* *No follow-up included.*
This is the low-friction entry product.

### 2. Plan Nutrirutina — 300.000 COP
This should be the main product.
**Positioning:** A complete personalized nutrition and execution plan made for real life.
**Includes:**
* Full intake review
* Personalized meal plan
* Breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snack options
* Food substitutions
* Weekly organization guide
* Grocery/shopping guide
* Practical recommendations based on schedule, budget, and cooking availability
* 1 minor adjustment after delivery
This is the product you want most people to buy. I’d make this the “recommended” tier.

### 3. Plan Acompañamiento — 500.000 COP
For people who know they need help staying consistent.
**Positioning:** Plan + follow-up + adjustments.
**Includes:**
* Everything in Plan Nutrirutina
* 2 weeks of WhatsApp follow-up
* 2 adjustments during the process
* Weekly check-in
* Support with difficulties: cravings, schedule problems, meals outside home, family meals, etc.
This is where her actual experience in follow-up and patient/user management becomes valuable.

### 4. Plan Transformación Realista — 850.000 COP
This is the premium tier.
**Positioning:** One-month personalized accompaniment.
**Includes:**
* Full personalized plan
* 4 weeks of follow-up
* Weekly adjustment/check-in
* Grocery guide
* Eating-out guide
* Family/home adaptation suggestions
* Progress review
* Final maintenance recommendations
This tier won’t sell as often, but it makes the 500k plan feel reasonable and gives serious clients a better option.

## Upsells
These are important because they let you increase ticket size without creating too many main plans.

* **Add-on 1: Extra adjustment — 50.000 COP**
  Ajuste adicional del plan por cambios de gustos, horarios, presupuesto o dificultad de ejecución.
* **Add-on 2: Guía de compras personalizada — 60.000 COP**
  Lista de compras organizada por grupos de alimentos y opciones prácticas.
* **Add-on 3: Menú semanal adicional — 80.000 COP**
  Una semana adicional de opciones de desayuno, almuerzo, cena y meriendas.
* **Add-on 4: Plan familiar — +120.000 to 200.000 COP per extra person**
  Example: Agrega un familiar al plan desde 150.000 COP adicionales. Useful for couples, mothers, families, or people who cook for multiple people.
* **Add-on 5: Seguimiento por WhatsApp — 150.000 COP / 2 weeks**
  Seguimiento, resolución de dudas y ajustes menores durante 2 semanas.
* **Add-on 6: Sesión virtual de orientación — 80.000 to 120.000 COP**
  A 30–45 minute video call. WhatsApp-first is more scalable, but this can be useful.
